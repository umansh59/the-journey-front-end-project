"Naruto" is a Japanese manga and anime series created by Masashi Kishimoto. It follows the journey of Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja with dreams of becoming the Hokage, the strongest ninja and leader of his village. Throughout its run, "Naruto" has captivated audiences with its intricate storytelling, memorable characters, and themes of friendship, perseverance, and self-discovery
